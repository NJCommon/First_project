Who is Batman?

Batman is the Dark Knight, a crime-fighting vigilante who protects Gotham City. Using intelligence, combat skills, detective abilities, and advanced technology, he strikes fear into criminals while upholding his vow to never take a life.

His mission began after witnessing the tragic death of his parents, which inspired him to dedicate his life to justice, training his body and mind to the peak of human ability.
