The Darkest Batman

The Batman Who Laughs is a twisted version of Batman from the Dark Multiverse, created when Batman is infected with the Joker toxin, turning him into a combination of Batman’s intelligence and Joker’s insanity.

He becomes one of the most dangerous villains in the DC universe, capable of outsmarting and overpowering even the strongest heroes.
