Most Known Robins

Batman has trained several partners throughout his crime-fighting career. These Robins have become heroes in their own right.

The most famous Robins are: Dick Grayson, Jason Todd, Tim Drake, and Damian Wayne. Each one brings their own skills, personality, and legacy to the role.
